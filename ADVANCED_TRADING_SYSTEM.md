## ðŸ”§ Technical Analysis Components

### 1. **Market Structure Analysis**
- **Higher Timeframe Bias (HTF)**: Determines overall market direction from 4H timeframe
- **Lower Timeframe Structure (LTF)**: Confirms entry signals from 1H timeframe
- **Trend Identification**: Bullish, Bearish, or Sideways market conditions
- **Trend Strength**: Measured using ADX, momentum, and volume confirmation

### 2. **Break of Structure (BOS)**
- **Bullish BOS**: Price breaks above recent swing high
- **Bearish BOS**: Price breaks below recent swing low
- **Strength Calculation**: Based on percentage move beyond structure
- **Confirmation**: Requires 0.1% buffer to avoid false signals

### 3. **Change of Character (CHoCH)**
- **Trend Reversal Detection**: Identifies when market structure changes
- **Bullish CHoCH**: Break of previous lower high pattern
- **Bearish CHoCH**: Break of previous higher low pattern
- **Early Warning System**: Catches trend changes before they fully develop

### 4. **Fair Value Gaps (FVG)**
- **Imbalance Detection**: Identifies gaps in price action
- **Bullish FVG**: Gap between candle lows and highs (upward bias)
- **Bearish FVG**: Gap between candle highs and lows (downward bias)
- **Fill Probability**: Tracks which gaps are likely to be filled

### 5. **Support & Resistance Levels**
- **Dynamic Level Detection**: Identifies key price levels from swing points
- **Proximity Analysis**: Calculates distance to nearest levels
- **Bounce/Rejection Signals**: Predicts reactions at key levels
- **Level Strength**: Based on number of touches and volume

### 6. **Supply & Demand Zones**
- **Zone Creation**: Identifies areas where strong moves originated
- **Demand Zones**: Areas where buying pressure emerged (bullish bias)
- **Supply Zones**: Areas where selling pressure emerged (bearish bias)
- **Zone Strength**: Based on the magnitude of the original move

### 7. **Traditional Indicators (Confirmation)**
- **RSI**: Momentum confirmation (oversold/overbought)
- **MACD**: Trend direction confirmation
- **EMA**: Moving average trend alignment
- **Stochastic**: Entry timing optimization
- **ADX**: Trend strength measurement
